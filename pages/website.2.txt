General Operations


The jurisdictional District of the Port of Alsea includes the Cities of Waldport, Yachats 

and unincorporated Tidewater. Within the definition of its own mission statement, the 

Port manages a fee boat ramp, moorage, large parking facilities and several upland leases 

on real properties wholly-owned by the Port.

	The Operations of the Port of Alsea are an indigenously cooperative endeavor of 

policy and actions necessarily harmonizing with local Chambers of Commerce, City 

Councils, the Oregon State marine Board, County Officials, the Oregon Public Ports 

Association(OPPA), as well as Business Oregon, Travel Oregon and the very 

interconnected Oregon Department of State Lands/the Oregon Department of Fish and 

Wildlife/U.S. Army Corps of Engineers relationship.
